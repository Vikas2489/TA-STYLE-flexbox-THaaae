CHRIS BUMSTEAD

Bumstead made his competitive debut aged 19 in 2014 and obtained his IFBB pro card aged 21 after claiming the 2016 IFBB North American Bodybuilding Championship.[4] After multiple shows, Bumstead was the runner-up of the 2017 and 2018 Mr. Olympia in the Classic Physique division, being edged out by Breon Ansley in both occasions.[5][6] Bumstead rose to international fame as he reached the top of the podium in 2019, 2020 and 2021.
